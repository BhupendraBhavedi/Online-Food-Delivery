Bulk Ordering

Bulk purchasing or mass buying is the purchase of much larger quantities than the usual, for a unit price that is lower than the usual. Wholesaling is selling goods in large quantities at a low unit price to retail merchants.Buying in bulk means buying large quantities of one item at once for a reduced overall cost per item. For example, you buy ten boxes of batteries together for a fraction of their individual sale price. Or you purchase oversized boxes of cereal for a lower cost per ounce than a smaller size box of cereal.
